Going beyond recycling: Our sustainability commitment
01. Green Recycle Deal
From Trash to Treasure: Repurpose your old chairs with us

We provide a hassle-free solution for customers to dispose of their old chairs while buying new or refurbished ones. We collect the old chairs during delivery of the new chair, and assess their economic value at our warehouse. We refurbish or upcycle the chairs or parts into other products, and keep spare parts in stock to refurbish other chairs.

Our sustainability commitment drives us to contribute to a circular economy and reduce waste. Join us in taking action for a better future - the Decade of Action starts now.

A greener alternative: Buy refurbished with confidence and give our chairs our second life

And guess what? You can be a part of it too! While we love to offer you the latest and greatest in new furniture, we also take pride in bringing you products that deserve a second life. Our selection includes items with little use, as well as those with minor damage that doesn't affect their functionality
